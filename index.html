Meet Dr. Prabin Dhakal – HOD of Dermatology

Dr. Prabin Dhakal is a highly qualified medical professional serving as Head of Department (HOD) of Dermatology at Manmohan Memorial Medical College and Teaching Hospital. He specializes in Dermatovenereology and Aesthetics, with a fellowship in Laser and Aesthetic Surgery from Delhi, India (2020).

His expertise includes diagnosis and management of skin, nail, and hair disorders including acne, eczema, psoriasis, vitiligo, fungal infections, and hair loss. He has special interest in STDs (syphilis, gonorrhea, herpes, HPV) and is proficient in laser and cosmetic surgery for skin rejuvenation, scar revision, hair removal, and pigmentation disorders.
